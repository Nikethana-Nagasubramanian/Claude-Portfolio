Design truths I'll carve on my tombstone

🏆 Form follows function

I follow this religiously, from product to building screens. Nailing the function helps me nail the form.

🖊️ Design with intention

After designing a screen, I pause and ask: What's the core action here? If any element doesn't serve that purpose, I cut it. Subtraction is a design tool — if it doesn't justify its spot, it's gone.

⏱️ 8-Sec Gut Check

I show the design to someone who's not involved actively in design for 8 seconds. Whatever they remember tells me what's working. If it doesn't align with the screen's goal, I go back and refine.

✏️ Whiteboard first, always

Whiteboarding is where ideas breathe. It's my go-to for unlocking flow, alignment, and fast problem-solving — every time.
